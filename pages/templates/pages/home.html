What is JamSession Lab?

We organise open jam sessions where musicians can meet, collaborate, and perform in a welcoming, supportive environment. Whether you play guitar, drums, vocals, or anything in between: there is always a spot for you on stage.

Open to all levels

From first-time performers to seasoned players, everyone is welcome to join in and learn from one another.

Events across Dublin

Regular sessions in Dublin and beyond, hosted at venues that celebrate live music and community spirit.

A growing community

Connect with fellow musicians, share your favourite genres, and build lasting friendships through music.
