What is JamSession Lab

JamSession Lab is a weekly open jam session designed to bring musicians and audiences together through live collaboration, improvisation, and creative exchange. The event runs every week and is structured to provide continuous live music from start to finish, creating a dynamic and engaging atmosphere for the venue. It's open to all instruments, styles, and skill levels, with a strong focus on inclusivity, musical growth, and community building.

Instead of fixed band performances, individual musicians are mixed into rotating groups guided by the organisers. This format encourages interaction, new musical connections, and a fresh live music experience every week.

{# About the Event #}

About the Event

The event typically runs from 19:00, with a flexible end time fully aligned with the venue's licensing and noise regulations. The format is designed to maintain a steady flow of live music throughout the entire night.

  • Short solo artist slots for original material
  • Weekly rotating jam sessions with mixed musicians
  • Final open jam session for all participants
  • Friendly, inclusive, and non-competitive atmosphere
