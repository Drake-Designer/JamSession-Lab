What is JamSession Lab

JamSession Lab is where musicians jump on stage, try things out, and make a night of it with a crowd that loves live music as much as we do. All instruments, all styles, all levels: if you want to play, you are welcome.

Forget the usual fixed-band setlist. We mix people into rotating groups, keep the music flowing, and leave room for surprises. Come for a jam, leave with new mates and a story worth telling.

About the Event

The event typically runs from 19:00, with a flexible end time fully aligned with the venue's licensing and noise regulations. The format is designed to maintain a steady flow of live music throughout the entire night.

  • Short solo artist slots for original material
  • Rotating jam sessions with mixed musicians
  • Final open jam session for all participants
  • Friendly, inclusive, and non-competitive atmosphere
